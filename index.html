Francisco Jose Luongo

Arnold O. Beckman Postdoctoral Fellow

California Institute of Technology

Biography

I am interested in how the visual system builds useful representations for segmenting and recognizing objects. I use a combination of large ensemble recordings of neural activity, statistical analyses, and modeling towards the goal of understanding the series of transformations that underlie visual perception. I am currently a postdoctoral scholar with Doris Tsao at Caltech.

Previously I was a graduate student with Vikaas Sohal at UCSF, where I investigated networks dynamics in prefrontal microcircuits and in chronic intracranial (ECoG) recordings from epileptic patients as part of the DARPA SUBNETS project.

I am currently funded by the Arnold and Mabel Beckman foundation and the Burroughs Wellcome Fund.
